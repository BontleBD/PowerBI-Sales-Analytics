# Power BI Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of customer conversion, product performance, organizational contribution, and sales trends using Microsoft Power BI.

The goal of this analysis is to identify key business insights and provide data-driven recommendations to improve conversion rates, optimize marketing strategies, and enhance overall business performance.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze customer conversion rates
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Evaluate organizational performance
- Understand sales trends over time
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Executive Summary
The analysis reveals:
- Inconsistent conversion rates across different time periods  
- Strong dependence on a few high-performing products  
- Variations in organizational effectiveness  
- Fluctuations in monthly sales trends  

These insights highlight opportunities to improve conversion efficiency, diversify product performance, and optimize partnerships.

---

## Conversion Analysis

### Key Findings
- A portion of customers subscribe but do not complete purchases  
- This indicates a gap between customer interest and actual conversion  

### Monthly Trends
- Conversion rates fluctuate across months  
- Peak performance observed in certain periods (e.g., April)  
- Declines occur in months such as May, June, and late-year periods  

### Insights
- Customer engagement is inconsistent  
- Evidence of seasonality in purchasing behaviour  
- Lower conversion periods may indicate ineffective marketing campaigns  

---

## Product Performance Analysis

### Top Products
- A small number of products generate the majority of purchases  
- These products dominate overall sales performance  

### Category Performance
- Certain categories contribute more significantly to total sales  
- Indicates higher demand in specific product segments  

### Insights
- Sales are concentrated around key products  
- Product demand is uneven across categories  
- Opportunity exists to expand performance across more products  

---

## Organizational Performance

### Key Findings
- Some organizations generate a high number of leads  
- High-volume organizations do not always convert effectively  

### Insights
- Lead quality differs across organizations  
- High quantity does not always equal high quality  
- Some partnerships may not be delivering optimal value  

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Improve Conversion Rates
- Implement targeted email follow-ups within 30 days  
- Use personalized offers and discounts  
- Introduce retargeting campaigns  

### Leverage High-Performing Products
- Increase marketing budget for top-performing products  
- Create bundled offers  
- Promote best-selling items more aggressively  

### Optimize Organizational Performance
- Review partnership agreements  
- Improve lead qualification processes  
- Focus on high-converting organizations  

### Address Sales Fluctuations
- Conduct A/B testing on marketing campaigns  
- Launch promotions during low-performing periods  
- Re-engage inactive customers
